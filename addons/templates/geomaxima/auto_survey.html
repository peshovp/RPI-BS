How It Works

Auto Survey-In determines precise base station coordinates by:

  1. Collecting FIX solutions from RTKLIB over 24 hours
  2. Filtering quality epochs (Q=1, ratio ≥3.0, 3D std ≤5cm)
  3. Removing outliers using Modified Z-Score (MAD-based)
  4. Computing weighted average position (inverse variance weighting)
  5. Applying geoid correction for orthometric height (MSL)
  6. Updating RTKBase configuration hourly
⚠️ Important:
  • Ensure clear sky view (minimal obstructions)
  • Do NOT move antenna during survey
  • Survey can recover from system restarts
  • Target accuracy: <5mm horizontal, <10mm vertical
